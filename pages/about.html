About Us

King James School of Nursing & Midwifery

This is an educational institution that trains individuals to become Nurses and Midwives

King James School of Nursing and Midwifery, located in Lira, Uganda, is a reputable institution offering certificate programs in nursing and midwifery. Recognized among Uganda's top-performing private nursing schools, it provides students with practical training through its affiliation with Lira Regional Referral Hospital. The school aims to produce competent and committed comprehensive nurses, contributing significantly to the healthcare sector
